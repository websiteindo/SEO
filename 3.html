Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time of year for big game fishing in Northland?

The best time for big game fishing in Northland is typically from December to May, when species like marlin and tuna are most abundant.

Are fishing charters suitable for beginners?

Yes, many private fishing charters in Northland are beginner-friendly and provide all the necessary gear and guidance to ensure a great experience for first-time anglers.

What should I bring on a private fishing charter?

It's recommended to bring sun protection, snacks, drinks, and appropriate clothing. Most charters provide all fishing gear, bait, and tackle.

How long do private fishing charters typically last?

Private fishing charters can last anywhere from a half-day (4-6 hours) to full-day trips (8+ hours), with some offering multi-day options.

Can I keep the fish I catch?

Many charters allow you to keep the fish you catch, although some practice catch-and-release for conservation purposes. Check with your charter operator for their specific policy.
